About Me

UI/UX Designer!

Hey there, I'm YuFeng a fourth-year student studying Intrative Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University. From an early age, I've been fascinated by the fusion of technology and design, and I've always sought ways to enhance user experiences. Through my studies and practical experiences, I've honed my eye for aesthetics and developed a deep passion for crafting seamless digital interactions. I'm eager to stay ahead of emerging trends and collaborate with peers in this ever-evolving field. My ultimate goal is to work with a tech company that values user-centric design, creating products that leave a lasting impact and delight users worldwide. As graduation nears, I'm excited to embrace new challenges and make my mark in the world of UX/UI design.
